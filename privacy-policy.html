Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5/1/25

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and protected when you visit this personal portfolio website.

1. Information Collected

This website does not collect any personal information from visitors directly. However, it uses Google Analytics to gather anonymous usage data.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics collects anonymous information about your visit, such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on each page
  • Browser and device type
  • General location (based on IP address)

This data helps me understand how the site is used and improve its content and layout. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored.

To learn more, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Use of Information

The information collected via Google Analytics is used solely for analytics purposes to improve the performance and usability of the website.

3. Data Sharing

No personal data is collected, and therefore, no data is shared with third parties apart from anonymized usage data shared with Google Analytics.

4. Your Rights

You may disable cookies or use a browser plugin to prevent Google Analytics from tracking your visit. For more information on opting out, visit Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Contact
